The Game of Life

:｡･:*:･ﾟ’★,｡･:*:･ﾟ’☆ RULES:

  • any live cell with two or three live neighbors survives
  • any dead cell with three live neighbors becomes a live cell
  • all other live cells die in the next generation. similarly, all other dead cells stay dead


    • :｡･:*:･ﾟ’★,｡･:*:･ﾟ’☆ HOW TO PLAY:
    ☆ press 'space' to pause/start the game
    ☆ click on the individual buttons to turn them "on" and "off"
    ☆ press 'c' to clear the grid
    ☆ REMEMBER to press 'space' to pause the game after clearing the grid so you can click on the buttons
    Have fun playing! ᕕ( ᐛ )ᕗ
